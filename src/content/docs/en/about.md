### Philosophy

I believe in **conscious C++** — understanding what happens between your source code and the
silicon. Every instruction, every cache line, every branch prediction matters when you're
building systems that process millions of events per second.

> "The bottleneck isn't the CPU. It's the path between the CPU and memory."

I write about this on my blog and share daily on [X](https://x.com/dionisiodev), where I've
published over 10,000 posts and built an audience of 8,000+ developers.
