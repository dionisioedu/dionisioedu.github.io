## What you will find here

This portal is designed to support real developer work:

- **reliable foundations** you can study without jumping between random resources
- **technical articles** with opinion, context, and practical application
- **real projects** with decisions, trade-offs, and next actions
- **career shortcuts** for resumes, study direction, and professional growth

<Aside type="note" title="Portal goal">
The goal is not just to publish content. It is to help you decide **what to study, what to reference, and where to grow next**.
</Aside>
