## What this hub is

This is a living engineering portal: a single place for **how things work**, **why decisions were made**, and **how to ship fast without chaos**.

Use it when you want:

- **One source of truth** for project setup and maintenance
- **Decision logs** that explain trade-offs (so future-you doesn’t redo debates)
- **Playbooks** you can apply today (copy/paste, checklists, commands)
- **Standards** that keep velocity high and quality consistent

<Aside type="note" title="Principle">
If a future-me could lose **30 minutes** on something again, it deserves a doc.
</Aside>

## How I keep this portal useful

- I keep pages **short and practical**
- I prefer **checklists** over essays
- I document **decisions with trade-offs**
- I keep a visible **“Next actions”** section in project pages
